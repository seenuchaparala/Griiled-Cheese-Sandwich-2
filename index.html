My grilled cheese sandwich recipe

Ingredients

  1. Bread
  2. Cheese
  3. Chicken slices
  4. Butter

Cooking process

  • Generously spread the butter on one side of two slices of bread
  • Pre-heat a pan on medium heat
  • Place one slice of bread on the pan with the spread facing down
  • Place one slice of cheese, two slices of chicken and another slice of cheese in that order
  • Flip the sandwich after 2 minutes
  • Serve the sandwich
